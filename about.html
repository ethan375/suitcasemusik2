Our Story

Created in 2015, Zero Equals One Equals Infinity is a registered 501c3 that entertains and educates audiences with a live and interactive program dubbed Suitcase Musik. Our mission is to facilitate raw expression through questions, stories, jokes and songs. Our unique, interactive musical experiences are tailor made to every guest who appears on the show.

Anyone can do it!

Come one, come all, but not all at once! Step on up, sit right down and be the next guess on the Suitcase Musik Show! Win fabulous prizes from our sweet sponsors as you get interviewed and help create an original song. Anyone can do it!
